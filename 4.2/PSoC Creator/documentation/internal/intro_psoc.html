What is PSoC®?

PSoC is Cypress's scalable Programmable System-on-Chip platform for 8-, 16-, and 32-bit applications. PSoC combines user-programmable precision analog and digital logic with high performance MCU sub-systems.

Cypress's new PSoC3 and PSoC5 families offer the world's first fully programmable embedded platform, delivering unmatched flexibility, integration, and performance.

  • Flexibility
  • Integration
  • Performance
  • Programmable Analog and Digital building blocks
  • Pre-configured and pre-characterized analog and digital peripherals
  • Seamless migration between 8-, 16-, and 32-bit systems
  • Any GPIO to any peripheral routing
  • Industry leading analog IP portfolio
  • Powerful programmable logic based digital system
  • Wide range of communication interfaces
  • Industry's most popular CPU options (ARM, 8051)
  • Robust power system with integrated boost
  • Low active and sleep power consumption
  • Feature rich, high performance peripherals
  • Precision clocking, precision analog ref
  • High speed CPU cores (4-80 MIPs)
